USER INSTRUCTIONS:
	1. Click on ship to place.
	2. Click the key 'r' to rotate ship if you desire.
	3. Click spot on your board (right) to place ship.
	4. Once all 5 ships are placed, alternate attacking on opponent board (left).
	5. First one to sink all the ships wins!.
